Peek Illustrations

These illustrations were made for a prototype of an app called Peek that aims to compare prices and save money and time. The intention is to reproach users and thus bring them closer to the App. The persons illustrations are presented as a presentation of the application and its functionality as soon as you open the App for the first time. The food illustration can be used as page layout, advertisements and etc.

Graphic Design

Peek

Peek was a prototype of a group-developed App to facilitate the price comparison process in supermarkets. The user would make their shopping list and the app would show the market at the nearest lowest overall cost. With this, the app would help save money and time in the users life.

Meta

This project was developed in a redesign process of the Forum of Metodologias Ativas and Tecnologia na Education (MeTa) which aims to discuss and disseminate the use of active methodologies and technologies in higher education, sharing experiences of teachers, researchers and educators.

TRATTATIVA

TRATTATIVA is a private conflict resolution chamber that conducts negotiations and mediations in which the objective is the settlement of through extrajudicial agreements between the parties involved. It is an alternative created 2017 by the National Council of Justice, to facilitate conflict resolution and help the judiciary. The idea is freedom of will between the parties, that is, willingness to resolve your conflicts in whatever way you think is fair and possible.
