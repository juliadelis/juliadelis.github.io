Illustrations

Mercurio

This illustration was a personal project of mine that I did based on a horse that I liked a lot when I was a teenager. His name was Mercurio and he had a very hot temper and so all the color palettes chosen are warm hues.

Peek Illustrations

These illustrations were made for a prototype of an app called Peek that aims to compare prices and save money and time. The intention is to approach users and thus bring them closer to the App. The caracters illustrated are used as a presentation of the App when its opened for the first time. The food illustration can be used as page layout, advertisements and etc.

Graphic Design

Peek

Peek was a prototype of a group-developed App to facilitate the price comparison process in supermarkets. The user would make their shopping list and the app would show the market at the nearest lowest overall cost. With this, the app would help save money and time.

Meta

This project was developed in a redesign process of the Forum de Metodologias Ativas and Tecnologia na Education (MeTa) which aims to discuss and disseminate the use of active methodologies and technologies in higher education, sharing experiences of professors, researchers and educators.

TRATTATIVA

TRATTATIVA is a private conflict resolution chamber that conducts negotiations and mediations in which the objective is the settlement of extrajudicial agreements between the parties involved. It is an alternative created 2017 by the National Council of Justice, to facilitate conflict resolution and help the judiciary. The idea is freedom of will between the parties, that is, willingness to resolve your conflicts in whatever way you think is fair and possible.
